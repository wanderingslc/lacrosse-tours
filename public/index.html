Come See Professional Lacrosse!

Come see Professional Lacrosse!

SplitDodgeTours has created a lacrosse based immersion experience centered around a professional lacrosse game.

Tours travel by the lacrosse fun bus to Denver from SLC. On the trip to and from riders are treated to games, lacrosse gear giveaways, videos and demonstrations.

At the destination you will have events such as lacrosse clinics, autograph sessions, access to local retail stores and back of house access to national equipment suppliers. At the stadium in addition to VIP treatment you will meet with player's and have field access dinner and premium seating. While staying close to the stadium in downtown to have access to its unique qualities, all for an affordable price.

Questions? Please get in touchor you can find out more about who's behind the tour here or read about us on Utah Lacrosse News !
